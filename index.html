MY FAVOURITE MEAL RECIPE

NOODLES

Noodle, a cooked egg-and-flour paste prominent in European and Asian cuisine, generally distinguished from pasta by its elongated ribbonlike form. Noodles are commonly used to add body and flavour to broth soups. They are commonly boiled or saut√©ed and served with sauces and meats or baked in casseroles. Asian noodles are made primarily from starch ingredients other than flour, although the recipes for some Asian pan-fried and stir-fried dishes allow the substitution of wheat-flour noodles for the Chinese mein. Noodles made from rice flour or the starch of the mung bean are softened in water and then boiled and drained in preparation for being fried or added to soups.

INGREDIENTS & MEASUREMENTS

INGREDIENTS MEASUREMENTS
Parboiled fresh noodles 250 gm
Chopped, grated cabbage 1 cup
Sliced union 1 cup
Powdered black pepper 1 teaspoon
Sunflower oil 2 tablespoon
Garlic paste 3 teaspoon
Finely chopped bean 1 cup
Eggs 3
Grated carrot 1 cup
Sliced green chilli 7
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Ginger paste 3 teaspoon
Ajinomoto 1 teaspoon
Soy sauce 2 tablespoon
Chopped coriander leaves 1 handfuls

STEPS OR METHOD

  1. To make this easy recipe, begin with par-boiling the noodles. Once done, drain the excess water and keep it aside.
  2. In the meantime, heat a pan over a medium flame.
  3. Once the oil is sufficiently hot, add the ginger-garlic paste and fry for some time.
  4. If you like to make it more delicious, add some finely chopped green chillies. You can also use chillies soaked in vinegar.
  5. Then add the onions, beans and green chillies in the same pan and saute for a minute or two.
  6. Add the carrots, cabbage, salt, pepper powder, soy sauce and Ajinomoto. Stir-fry the ingredients for some time.
  7. Once done, add the boiled noodles and break the eggs into the pan. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  8. If you like smoky flavours, stir the veggies mix on a high flame.
  9. Cover the pan with a lid and allow it to cook for around 2-3 minutes.
  10. Once done, transfer the dish to a serving bowl and garnish it with coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot and pair it with a spicy gravy of your choice.
